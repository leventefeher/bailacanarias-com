Bachata is a genre of music and social dance originating from the Dominican Republic. Nowadays, Bachata is a global phenomenon danced all around the world.

Bachata has evolved from bolero, son and merengue and is usually danced with partners. The music is characterised by its distinctive guitar sound and the way the rhythm guides your movement.

## Styles of Bachata

There are several styles of Bachata danced today:

- **Traditional / Dominican Bachata** — the original style, staying closest to the music's roots
- **Bachata Moderna** — a more modern, internationally influenced style
- **Bachata Sensual** — a style characterised by body movement and connection
